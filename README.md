# ⚖️ CJ-Official | Cyber Justice Organization

> **"IN CODE WE TRUST. IN JUSTICE WE SERVE."**

Welcome to the official profile of **Cyber Justice (CJ)**. We are a specialized technical entity focused on auditing, security research, and digital community integrity.

---

### 🛡️ Who We Are
Cyber Justice is dedicated to protecting developers and users from malicious actors and "skidding" culture. We promote transparency, high-quality software standards, and ethical hacking principles.

### 🎯 Our Mission
*   **Security Auditing:** Identifying and reporting vulnerabilities in community-driven software.
*   **Educational Support:** Training new developers to build sustainable and ethical code.
*   **Community Safety:** Maintaining a "Blacklist" of malicious entities and verified loggers to protect the community.

---

### 🏛️ Governance & Roadmap
Our organization operates under a strict **Codex** to ensure technical excellence:
*   **Supreme Authority:** Led by the Founder (@Dannekz) and Technical Overseer (@Pagoni-Hax).
*   **Current Status:** Operating as an independent technical collective in the **Development Phase**.
*   **Future Goals:** Formalizing our legal and non-profit structure in Sweden (Scheduled for Summer 2026).

---

### 🛠️ Technical Excellence
*   **OPSEC First:** Mandatory 2FA, data encryption, and strict confidentiality protocols.
*   **Quality Control:** No code reaches the 'Main' branch without passing internal vulnerability assessments and automated testing.
*   **The Pagoni Doctrine:** Commitment to mentorship and training junior members.

---

### 📢 Institutional Identity
*   **Location:** Based in Sweden 🇸🇪.
*   **Ethics:** We remain neutral in community drama unless the organization’s safety is at stake.
*   **Contact:** For official inquiries or reporting breaches, use approved internal channels.
